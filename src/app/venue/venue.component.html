Recreational activities

Discover the legendary Bran Castle, often associated with the Dracula myth. Nestled in the Carpathian Mountains, this historic fortress offers a glimpse into Romania's rich medieval history. Peles Castle, with its stunning Neo-Renaissance architecture, is a masterpiece of design and craftsmanship. The Antipa Museum in Bucharest is a treasure trove of natural history and biodiversity. Its extensive collections and interactive exhibits make it a fascinating destination for all ages. The Horia Hulubei National Institute of Physics and Nuclear Engineering is a center of excellence in physics and nuclear engineering. It's renowned for its cutting-edge research and contributions to science.
